# Brand Strategy

## Positioning

Ugly Actions makes premium training wear for the unglamorous work behind visible progress.

## Voice

- Direct
- Technical
- Confident
- Slightly raw, but never sloppy

## Design Direction

- Black and graphite base
- White/fog text
- Volt accent for action and focus
- Signal orange as a secondary energy color
- Tight layouts, strong product surfaces, and clear calls to action

## Temporary Identity

Use the wordmark "Ugly Actions" until a real logo is designed. Logo concepts belong in `artifacts/brand/logo-concepts/`.
